A very simple example of the Canonical Navigation Banner to facilitate screenshots of it.

Instructions:

  1. Edit the banner text in the simple-nav-banner.html file to your liking:
    2. <span class="p-navigation__logo-title">

</span>
  2. Refresh the page
  3. Take a screenshot of just the navigation bar inside the dashed border
  4. Crop the image if needed and use wherever you'd like!
  5. N.B. You can also change the width of the banner by resizing your browser window
